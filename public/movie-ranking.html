My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Interstellar

Directed by Christopher Nolan, Interstellar is a mind-bending sci-fi epic that explores space travel, time dilation, and the survival of humanity. The film's stunning visuals and emotional depth make it a must-watch.

2. Forrest Gump

Starring Tom Hanks, Forrest Gump is a heartwarming tale of an extraordinary Man

3. Cars

Cars is a delightful animated film by Pixar that follows the journey of a race car named Lightning McQueen.
